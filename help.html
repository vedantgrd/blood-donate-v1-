Frequently Asked Questions

How do I request blood in an emergency?

Call our 24/7 helpline at 1800-XXX-XXXX or fill out the form above. For emergencies, we prioritize requests and connect you with the nearest available donor.

Who can donate blood?

Generally, anyone who is at least 18 years old, weighs more than 50kg, and is in good health can donate blood. Specific eligibility criteria may vary.

How often can I donate blood?

You can donate whole blood every 3 months (12 weeks). Plasma can be donated more frequently, typically every 28 days.
